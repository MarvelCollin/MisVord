ROUTER PORT FORWARDING SETUP:

1. Buka router admin: 192.168.1.1 atau 192.168.0.1
2. Login dengan username/password router
3. Cari menu "Port Forwarding" atau "Virtual Server"
4. Tambah rule baru:

   Service Name: MisVord Web
   External Port: 80
   Internal IP: 10.20.176.105
   Internal Port: 80
   Protocol: TCP
   Status: Enable

5. Save dan restart router
